Before you use metal volume extension, please:

- Ensure that your circle or other curve have large number of segment for better volume result if you want calculate volume of entities.

- If you want to calculate volume of solid object, group it or make it as a component

- Ensure that you only choose one component before choose calculate metal volume

- Use texture in metal folder to mark your entities a metal object

- thickness default is 0.1cm and you can change in addjust face thickness
